Token distribution

The more Ether everyone contributes, the more SLUTs you get.

The base token distribution formula is very simple: for every 1 ETH you contribute, you'll receive 1000 SLUTs, and if you contribute at least 0.01 ETH, you'll also receive an additional 1 SLUT for every Ether that's been contributed in total. Like this:

Transaction Ether sent Total Ether sent SLUTs received
#1 1.000 ETH 1.000 ETH 101.000 UET
#2 3.000 ETH 4.000 ETH 304.000 UET
#3 0.005 ETH 4.005 ETH 0.500 UET
#4 0.500 ETH 4.505 ETH 54.505 UET

Bonus SLUTs!

Every block has a 1 in 256 chance of issuing bonus SLUTs.

When purchasing SLUTs, the smart contract will take a hash of the current block's coinbase, number, and timestamp. If the first byte of the hash is 0x00, the block becomes a bonus block. During a bonus block, the smart contract counts the number of bits set in the second byte of the hash and uses that number as a bonus multiplier.

Every SLUT purchase of at least 0.01 ETH worth during the bonus block will receive, in addition to the base distribution, an additional 100 UET per Ether multiplied by the bonus multiplier. That means that contributors have a chance of receiving a total of nine times the number of sluts they expected!
